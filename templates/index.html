    Hackers in Residence Program!

    Imagine a constant stream of diverse hackers from everywhere, traveling the world, gaining invaluable experience, sharing what they know and love, helping others everywhere they go.

    Imagine diverse Organizations all over the world offering Residency opportunities for hackers, where communities will greatly benefit from the wealth of knowledge and skills, enthusiasm and creativity hackers have to offer.

    This International Hackers in Residence site links cool people with cool organizations!

    Schools, universities, hackerspaces, libraries, arts communities, and other Organizations around the world, can create a free web page to describe their Hackers In Residence Program which will attract potential Residents.

    Anyone can browse Residency opportunities, and apply to Organizations where they would like to share of themselves, learn from others while there, and bring what they learn back home, and spread the joy.

    Everybody wins!

    This site is totally free for everyone to use.

    If you would like to be a Resident it is free to browse the list of Organizations to find Residency Opportunities to apply for.

    If you are an Organization it is free to register, enter info about your Organization, what you offer Residents, and what you require from Residents. {% endblocktrans %} {% endblock %}
